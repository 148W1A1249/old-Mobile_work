Verb practice

Select the verb .

That story about the disaster really did trouble him.

A helping verb comes before a main verb. It helps the main verb show action.

I am resting.

The helping verb is am. The main verb is resting.

Here are some common helping verbs:

forms of to be: am, are, is, was, were

forms of to have: has, have, had

forms of to do: do, does, did

modal verbs: can, could, may, might, will, would, shall, should, must

The helping verb always goes before the main verb, but the two verbs are not always next to each other.

I am finally resting.
